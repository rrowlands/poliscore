The World's First Legislative Interpretation Service

Interpreting Bills

It all starts with bill interpretations. Using AI, we score bills across policy areas according to various criteria. These scores are then aggregated and used to evaluate the performance of politicans.

The Wikipedia of Bill Interpretations

It is expected that bill interpretations will be divisive. This is why citizens will have the ability to submit their own intepretations of bills. Other citizens will then be able to upvote or downvote interpretations, and the most upvoted bill interpretations will be used in grading.

A Citizen's Dashboard

When it comes time to vote, we'll have a dashboard ready for you to review, showing you all the relevant politicians in your area and everything that they've been up to since you've been away. Leave the hard work of digging through bills to us!
